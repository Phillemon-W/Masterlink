INTERIOR DECOR

This service entails enhancing the interior of commercial buildings by decorating and furnishing them. Our company also provides provide unmatched solutions for design logistics of all kind.We leverage a single partner to oversee the pickup, consolidation, storage, and delivery of all items going into a space, so that you can gain control.

That means visibility into the big picture, less risk of damage, and lower costs of transport. You'll also get the added convenience of having one point of contact for all tracking, updates, and changes.

Services Offered

  • Design and decoration of the interior of commercial buildings.
  • Furnishing the interior of commercial buildings.
  • Comprehensive shipping service options nationally and internationally, including high value insurance, crating and more.
  • Inspection of each piece as it arrives to confirm condition; minor repair services included.

How It Works

We offer a dedicated account manager to serve as your single point-of-contact. We also have project management support to schedule and track logistics for all purchases (furniture, art, sculpture, fixtures, accessories, etc.). We have an extensive knowledge of best practice processes and solutions that will shape the way we interact and fulfill your design vision.

We excel in transporting items that are too big for a parcel store and too small for a large van line. Our carrier network is extensive and gives us the flexibility to create custom solutions for nearly any need. For nearly 30 years, Our Company has specialized in pickup and delivery of high end furniture and antiques.The company is driven by a focus on customer needs by providing integrated logistics solutions with operational excellence.
