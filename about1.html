    • ABOUT US

    Our Company-Destination Masterlink East African Limited was formed in the year 2015. It took over from former company called Economy Master Link(EA)ltd. The defunct Economy Masterlink (EA)ltd was wounded up due to leadership and shareholding wrangles. Due to the above issues the Destination Masterlink(EA)ltd was born.It’s a limited liability company by shares and registered under the companies act of Kenya constitution.It deals with distribution of goods from factory to client and factory to client depots countrywide. To bring services closer to people we normally offer door to door services.

    We specialize in local provision of logistic services and across border deliveries on special request or arrangements. Courier departments handles small and lighter parcels, within and around major towns of Kenya. Goods from one to ten tonnes are our main business.We deliver goods to all major towns of Kenya from one ten to ten tonnes.Having done deliveries for major companies and government institutions we have enough experience to handle any logistical challenge.We have a team of experienced personnel who can handle many challenges. Their education and experience in different field is an enough aspect in managing our company hence excellent service provision.
